What is Personalized Learning?


Personalized Learning is an innovative online bachelors degree program that offers an innovative way to pursue a traditional education in a non traditional way.


Personalized learning allows students to work at their own pace and choose the order in which they complete their lessons. This allows students to graduate earlier or later, depending on their peronal motivation and current life situation. Workers can spend their night earning a bachelors degree. Students can graduate faster and cheaper by working around the clock or whenever they have time.


The cost of the degree program is on par with community college tuiton, which is perfect for students who are just starting to build their wealth. It is an exceptional bargain that offers a real degree backed by a world class university


Unlike other online programs the degree is not designated with "distance learning", rather it appears identical to a traditional on campus degree. This means that in NAU's eyes the curriculum is equivalent in rigor to its on campus courses, therefore it deserves to be distinguished as such.

Why Choose Personalized Learning?



Personalized is an innovative program unlike any other. There are many reasons to pursue it, however the best reasons to choose Personalized Learning is as follows:


Affordable Tuition

Currently priced at $3000, Peronalized Learning offers a University Education at a community college price.


Graduate Faster

Personalized is completely self paced and is perfectly suited to match your lifestyle. When you graduate is up to you!


Fully Accredited

Personalized Learning is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission, just like the rest of the university.


Same Degree

A Personalized Learning degree is marked the same as any other NAU degree and doesn't specify "distance learning"

Personalized Learning Degree Programs

Computer Information Technology

The program is designed to provide the foundational skills for continuous learning and to adapt to the fast changing information technology environment. This program also includes competency-based and hands-on learning modules to enhance acquired business skills. Parts of the curriculum are designed around highly valued certifications. Students will also augment their IT skills

Small Business Administration

This major will provide you with knowledge, skills, and resources to start, manage, and grow a small business. Specifically, this program will help turn an initial business idea into a reality through a series of 'must-do' actions and learning activities. This program includes placement in an internship, where you will gain real-world experience in small business management. It includes a capstone project.

Liberal Arts

A student majoring in Liberal Arts through Personalized Learning will study broadly from history, philosophy, literature and the arts. The student will learn how to analyze, interpret and evaluate important cultural artifacts of diverse cultures. This is a fresh, contemporary take on Liberal Arts in that each exercise in understanding important works of art and letters is immediately connected to relevant skills.
